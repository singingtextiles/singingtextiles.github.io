Paola Torres Núñez del Prado

Lima, Perú - Based in Stockholm

Recipient of the Google Arts + Culture Artists + Machine Intelligence Grant (2019/20)

Her works are in the Public Art Agency, Sweden (2018)

and the Malmo Museum of Art, Sweden (2017)

Winner of the “Local Media: Amazon Ecoregion” contest of Vivo Arte.mov in Brazil (2013)

Her work has been exhibited in various cities in Sweden, in New York, New Mexico, and Massachusetts in the United States, in Bergen and Lofoten in Norway, in Luxembourg, in Nantes, France, in the D.F. Querétaro and Morelia, in Mexico, in London, in the United Kingdom, in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, in Belem, in Brazil, in Santiago, Chile, and also in multiple cities in Peru.
